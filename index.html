Act I

Who We Are

AARSHI envisions the promotion of classical theatre acting and various forms of performance — while bringing together cultures from across the country onto a single stage.

Beyond the stage lights, AARSHI stands as a family — a space built on trust, collaboration, learning, and shared memories. We nurture individuals both as performers and as people: building confidence, discipline, teamwork, and artistic freedom.

With every production, AARSHI seeks to inspire, provoke thought, and celebrate the timeless magic of theatre.

Act II

Flagship Events

Annual Competition

Mrignayanee

Our annual Online Summer Reel-Making Competition encouraging participants to explore storytelling, acting, editing, and cinematic creativity through short-form digital content. A platform to experiment with performance beyond the stage.

Annual Production

Drama Workshop & Production

Intensive training in acting, stage movement, voice modulation, improvisation, and production under experienced mentors — culminating in a full-fledged theatrical production before a live audience.

Inter-Batch

Rangabhumi

The Inter-Batch Drama Competition of IISER Kolkata, bringing together students from different batches to conceptualize, write, direct, and perform original theatrical productions.

Solo Performance

Abhivyakti

The Monodrama Competition of IISER Kolkata — celebrating the art of solo performance and serving as the selection platform for IISER Kolkata's representative at the IICM Monodrama Competition.
